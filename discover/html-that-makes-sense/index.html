How to make a chocolate cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk tea
  • 1 cup of sunflower or corn oil
  • 2 egg units
  • 2 cups of wheat flour tea
  • 1 cup of powdered chocolate tea
  • 1 cup of sugar tea
  • 1 tablespoons of baking powder

Method of preparation

  1. Put liquids in a blender and blend until well blended.
  2. Add the other ingredients, the yeast being the last.
  3. Take it to bake in a medium oven, in a greased and floured form.
  4. Once ready, make holes with a toothpick or fork in the cake and pour in chocolate milk.
  5. Finish with the chocolate ganache topping.
